What to study?

Our study program in Robotics is composed by several types of courses (OCW, MOOCs, etc), seminars and resources (OER) from different Internet sources; some examples of courses are:
  • C++ Programming
  • Introduction to Robotics
  • Mobile Robotics
  • Artificial Intelligence for Robotics (or How to Programming a Robotic Car)
  • Machine Learning
  • Artificial Intelligence Planning

You can find information about these and other courses of the program in our wiki.
